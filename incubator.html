Chicken Incubator

Fully Automated Hatching Machine

Project Overview

This incubator is the result of months of work, designed to automate the 21-day process of hatching chicks from eggs. The project's originality and design are entirely my own.

It is a fully autonomous system that controls every aspect of embryo development. It features precise temperature and humidity regulation systems to ensure an optimal environment. Furthermore, an automatic mechanism rotates the eggs periodically to prevent the embryo from sticking to the shell, mimicking natural incubation.

Technical Specs

  • Capacity: 112 Eggs
  • Temp: 37.3°C (Precise)
  • Humidity: 54% Constant
  • Auto-Rotation: Every 4 Hours (45°)
  • Cycle Duration: 21 Days
